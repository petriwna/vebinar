Cookies Policy

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and serve personalized content. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that enable us to recognize returning users and remember preferences, such as language and theme settings. We employ session cookies to keep your session active and secure, and persistent cookies to remember your choices over multiple visits. Third-party cookies from services like Google Analytics help us monitor site performance and optimize our content for you. You have the right to accept or decline cookies at any time by adjusting your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect certain features of the site and degrade your overall experience. We do not store personally identifiable information in cookies, and all data collected is anonymized. Our cookies do not link to any personal account information.
